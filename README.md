### 🧭 About me

I'm a **Senior Solutions Engineer** by day and an **indie developer** by night. After a decade leading people and solving problems, I moved into engineering with the same instinct: build things that actually help, ship them in public, and stay close to the people using them.

Lately I'm most interested in **AI-augmented tools for everyday workflows** — products that turn overwhelming processes (job searching, daily habits, customer messaging) into something approachable and a little fun.

📍 Antioch, CA &nbsp;•&nbsp; ☕ Always down to talk shop
